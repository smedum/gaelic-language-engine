Gaelic Language Engine

Preserving endangered languages by putting living speech first.

We build tools that make speaking and listening the foundation of learning. Starting with Scottish Gaelic, we're creating a model for language revitalization that values the human voice above all else.

Our Mission

Languages aren't lost when the last dictionary is burned. They're lost when the last conversation falls silent.

We focus on sound-first learning because speech is the living thread of culture. Every recording, every hesitant pronunciation from a new learner, is a act of preservation.
